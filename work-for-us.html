Join the Clear Waters Team

At Clear Waters Outfitting Company, we’re more than just an outdoor business—we’re a team passionate about helping people experience the water safely and confidently. Whether you're working directly with customers or behind the scenes in our shop, every role plays a key part in delivering high-quality service and unforgettable adventures.

Open Positions

Summer Team Member / Driver

We are a diverse paddlesport company. Our Summer Team Members help customers prepare for river trips, transport customers and deliver equipment.

  • Physically demanding role (must be able to lift 40–60 lbs)
  • Load and unload canoes, kayaks, and SUP boards
  • Transport customers and assist with water access
  • Clean and repair equipment
  • Yard maintenance and shop assistance
  • Assist with retail operations (sales, cashiering, restocking)
  • Answer phones and take reservations

Providing great customer service is essential. Drivers must have a clean driving record.

Repair Shop Technician Team Member

NorthWest Canoe is a full-service woodshop for canoe, kayak and SUP board repairs as well as wood strip boat building. Shop Technicians assist with a wide range of repair and fabrication work.

  • Repair wood strip, fiberglass, Kevlar, and composite canoes
  • Install skid plates, replace gunwales, refinish hulls
  • Install seats, thwarts, and custom components
  • Organize projects for shop efficiency
  • Work with power tools, wood, resins, varnishes, and cloth
  • Assist in building wood strip canoes
  • Transport canoes and maintain shop cleanliness

Experience with tools and materials is helpful but not required. We provide training for the right candidate. We’re looking for detail-oriented team members who take pride in quality workmanship.

We offer flexible schedules, competitive wages, and opportunities to expand your skills.
