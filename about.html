Why Our Location is Special

The Wild & Scenic Mississippi River

Designated "Wild & Scenic" by the DNR, many miles along this stretch are still in their natural state due to the protection, regulations, and zoning associated with this title. Because the Mississippi River is a major migratory corridor, paddlers often see eagles, blue herons, egrets and hawks along with many other birds, mammals, fish, and crustaceans that call this area home.

Here, the Mississippi River is shallow with a rocky bottom - poor conditions for motor boats, but ideal for paddling and fishing. With a gentle current void of any rapids, dams or falls, the paddling is relaxing and the fishing is spectacular.

Many consider this stretch to be world class smallmouth bass fishing due to the limited motor traffic, river accessibility, and special fishing regulations.

We are convinced that you'll enjoy this Midwest gem whether you are new to paddling or a seasoned veteran!
