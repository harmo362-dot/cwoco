Paddling Trips

Choose from scenic trips perfect for all skill levels.

5 Mile Trip

The 5-mile trips starts you at Island View Park. From there you paddle 5 miles down the river back to our location. During your trips you will pass Boy Scout Point, a DNR campsight, which is a perfect spot to stop and enjoy the river. This trip takes about 1-2 hours.

8 Mile Trip

The 8-mile trips starts at our location, and takes you down to Snuffy's Landing, where you will call us for a pickup. This stretch takes you past sandbars, and some notable slack water, which can be great for fishing. Toward the end of your trip, you will pass Oak Island, a DNR campsight in the middle of the river. This paddle takes about 3-4 hours.

13 Mile Trip

The 13-mile trips starts at Beaver Island Park near St. Cloud. You will paddle around all the Beaver Islands, pass by two DNR campsights, before finishing your trip at our location. This trip takes about 4-6 hours.

Lake Experience

If you are looking for somethiing other than the river, this is for you! We offer 2 or 4 hour lake paddles as Warner Lake Count Park. Warner Lake is a non-motirized lake, great for paddling and fishing. There is also a beach on the lake, great for picnicing.

Equipment Rentals

Rent canoes, recreational kayaks, fishing kayaks, and SUP boards for a fun day on the water. Perfect for personal adventures, family outings, or even the Bounday Waters!

Canoes

We offer a range of both recreational and performance canoes to meet different paddling needs. Our recreational canoes are well-suited for casual outings on rivers and lakes, providing stability and ease of use for paddlers of all experience levels. For more demanding adventures, our performance canoes are designed specifically for Boundary Waters travel. These high-performance models are constructed from Wenonah Kevlar, delivering a lightweight yet durable option ideal for extended trips and portaging.

Recreational Kayaks

Our recreational kayaks are designed to accommodate all skill levels, offering a stable and user-friendly experience for both beginners and experienced paddlers. We exclusively carry kayaks from Current Designs, a trusted name in quality and performance. Our fleet includes 10 ft, 12 ft, and 13.5 ft models, allowing customers to choose the size that best fits their comfort and paddling needs.

Fishing Kayaks

We offer a wide selection of fishing kayaks from trusted brands such as Old Town, NuCanoe, and Bonafide. Each model is designed to deliver stability, performance, and functionality on the water. If you have questions about which kayak best fits your needs, our team is available to provide expert guidance and help you make the right choice.

SUP Boards

We offer both hard boards and inflatable stand-up paddleboards (SUPs) within our rental fleet to accommodate a range of preferences and experience levels. All boards are from Grey Duck and are available in a variety of lengths, allowing customers to select the option that best suits their comfort and intended use on the water.
